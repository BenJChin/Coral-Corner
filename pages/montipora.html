Montipora

Montipora corals are considered the easiest of the small polyp stony (SPS) corals to keep. The first SPS corals for all new reef keepers are the Montipora species.

These attractive, velvety corals are tolerant of most water parameters including light and water flow. These are not aggressive corals, but are rather more submissive.
